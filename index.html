The Puck = Your Prompt: Just like dropping a puck starts the Plinko game, your prompt starts the AI's text generation process.
The Pegs = Decision Points: Each peg represents a moment where the AI chooses the next word. At every peg, the AI considers all possible words and picks one based on probability.
Temperature = Randomness: Low temperature (0.1) makes the AI very predictable - like a puck that barely bounces. High temperature (2.0) makes it very creative and unpredictable - like a super bouncy puck!
Prompt Type = Guidance: Basic prompts are vague and lead to scattered results. Detailed prompts with context and role information guide the AI toward more focused, useful responses - like adding bumpers to guide the puck!
Multiple Drops = Multiple Responses: Even with the same prompt, AI can generate different responses each time, just like how pucks can take different paths down the board.
The Slots = Final Response: Where the puck lands represents the final text the AI generates - one of many possible outcomes from the same starting point.
